Ultimate Frisbee: Complicated, But Fun

Ultimate Frisbee is a non-contact sport where a flying disc is the main use of it. Played as a mix team, or just a one-gender team, Ultimate Frisbee is open to many people of all age and skills. This exciting, but intense sport is played by many all over the world and is very fast-paced.

The Rules of Ultimate Frisbee

The main purpose of Ultimate Frisbee is to get the flying disc to the opposing side in order to get a point for your team. Played on a large rectangular field, the defense team tries to stop the offense team (the team with the disc) to get the disc to the other side. The offense (team that has possession of the disc) pass the disc to other players in their team and try to forward with the disc. No physical contact is permitted in a game, as it is called a foul.

Why YOU should Play Ultimate Frisbee

Although Ultimate Frisbee is a very complicated sport to achieve in a single year, the community in Ultimate Frisbee makes the sport a fun experience. It is a very unique sport with heavy-spirited sportsmanship that have taken skills from many other sports (like basketball and baseball) and combines it into one. It improves many skills at once and is a ton of fun when you play as a team.

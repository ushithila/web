Why YOU Should be Engaged in Ultimate Frisbee

Now that you have read everything about the basics that you need to know about Ultimate, allow me to convince you why you should play this one of a kind sport :).

Unlike other sports, Ultimate Frisbee has many, many, MANY tactical skills combined into one. This allows for players to gain more skills and get a piece of every sport in the whole wide world!

Along with the development of skills, the community in ultimate is very opening and friendly. Many of the people there are very warm and welcoming towards new players. Trust me on that; I am saying this from personal experience.

Last but not least, ultimate is literally, seriously, quite literally FUN! The community, the people, the games, everything about it (!) is why you should try Ultimate!
